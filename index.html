Bet: 10

Jack: 1000

Game Rules:

  • Game Rules:
    1. Players start by placing a bet using the slider. The bet cannot exceed the Jack balance.
    2. Press 'Roll' to roll a dice, the number rolled will be added to your score.
    3. If the first roll is 4 or less, the host wins immediately.
    4. Press 'Hold' to end your turn, and the host will roll until it beats your score or exceeds 100.
    5. If the host's score exceeds 100 or is less than the player's score after holding, the player wins double the bet.
    6. If the scores are tied after holding, it's a draw, and the player gets back the bet.
    7. The game resets after a short pause when a round ends.
